Bible Journey helps you connect a number (often the time you notice God highlighting) to a scripture reference and study notes, so you can reflect and respond.

  1. Notice a number (e.g., a time like 5:14 or a time you keep seeing).
  2. Type the number below and click Resolve.
  3. Choose a translation (KJV / ASV / WEB) to view the verse and study notes.
  4. Use My Journal to record your themes and reflection. (Saved locally on this device.)
